Application Process and Checklist for
Ph.D / Master of Science by Research Admissions

A summary / cheat-sheet of the full Call for Applications is given below. Please refer to this document for specific details. The call for applications overrides in case of any conflict.

  1. Registration - this will create a unique user id (your email) for you that you can use to access the site with, throughout the application process. This triggers an email to your registered email address.
  2. Click on the link in the registration email to activate your account. You may have to change your default password to something of your choice.
  3. Login with your email and password and pay the Non-Refundable Application Fee of ₹ {{ globals.currentCycle.application_fee }}/-. The Application fee is to be paid through the payment gateway FeePlaza that IIIT-B is partnering with. Please follow the link on the registration page of the application --- this will direct you to the Fee Plaza site where you can make your payment. The registration will be cancelled if you do not activate the account and complete the payment within 48 hrs after the registration.
  4. You can see the rest of the application on payment confirmation. Please fill the application completely. Application can be filled in parts and saved at your convenience.
  5. Fill in the details of at least 2 referees. They will be contacted directly by IIIT-B by email. The referees will requested to upload their recommendations directly on this portal.
  6. Submit the application before the deadline - {{ globals.currentCycle.application_deadline | date: 'MMM dd, yyyy; HH:mm' }} Hrs
  7. Watch this portal / your email for updates on your application.

The following checklist would help you be prepared with the application process. We suggest you keep the required information / documents ready before you start applying.


  • Personal Information (email, photograph, address, and some official identity proof)
  • Details of Academic Qualifications - from 10th to PG (if applicable). This must include institution, year of passing, marks/grades in each course/subject and a consolidated pdf of degree certificate along with the transcripts with all the marks and grades.
  • Details of Competitive Exams (GRE, GATE, JEST etc.) you may have taken. Some of these may be necessary for the specific program / domain that you may be applying to. Please check the Call for Applications for details. The details required would include exam name, score obtained and soft (scanned) copy of the score sheet.
  • Your choice of the research domain and specific area(s) in which you want to pursue research.
  • Statement of Purpose, Research Preparation documents that you are expected to write to let us know your motivation and preparedness for the research program that you are applying for. These must be uploaded as individual .pdf documents.
  • (Optional) Document (.pdf) detailing your work-experience if any.
  • (Optional) Document (.pdf or .doc) listing all your publications / patents if any along with soft copies of 2 representative publications of yours.
  • Email and contact details of at least 2 referees who can vouch for your research potential and credentials. Please note that the referees will be contacted directly by IIIT-B requesting them to upload their recommendations directly on the portal.
